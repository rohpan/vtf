Assignments Related Questions
When are weekly assignments due?

Assignments are due on Wednesday at 11:59 pm PST.

Due dates are also visible in your local time zone in google classroom per assignment. Optionally, you can click on google calendar to see assignment due dates from the classroom view.

I tried to access the form for the Weekly Certificate of Completion, but I received the message, "Certificate of Completion Information is no longer accepting responses. Try contacting the owner of the form if you think this is a mistake." Can I still get a Certificate of Complete for the previous week's work?
No. Requests for Certificates of Completion of the previous week's work are available for request until 11:59 pm PST the Monday of the following week. We recommend you continue with the current week's work and be sure to fill out the form for the following week. If you have extenuating circumstances and you require a certificate, you have the option of opening a ticket at help desk.
What If there is a school commitment that prevents me from completing assignments?
According to VT internship policies, every intern must complete assignments on time or will be disqualified. However, it is an at-will internship which means you can gracefully leave by creating a help desk ticket. You can always apply for future internships.
What if I turn in an assignment late?
You will need to state a reason for turning in your assignment late in the comments section. Late assignments incur a 10% penalty. If you do not state submit a reasonable explanation as to why your assignment was late, or if you do not state a reason, you will incur a 25% penalty. You will be allowed 1 late assignment per term.
What if I know I will need to turn in an assignment late due to extenuating circumstances?
Contact an intern-proctor with your issue prior to the due date.
When will VMware hands-on task assignments will start?
Stay tuned for weekly assignments as it publishes every week to only qualified interns who advance to next week.
Are weekly LIVE meetings mandatory?
No. LIVE meetings are another way of intern collaboration and getting your questions or doubts answered by support staff.
If my family member is testing positive for COVID-19, how can I stay in the internship program?

You can choose either a proactive or reactive exception option.

According to VT internship policies, every intern must complete assignments on time or will be disqualified. However, it is an at-will internship which means you can gracefully leave by creating a help desk ticket. You can always apply for future internships.

How can I get an extension on an assignment?

If you know ahead of time, you can proactively send a message to a proctor to let them know that your assignment will be late and the reason for the late submission. If you have already missed a due date, you will need to contact a proctor within 24 hours and provide a written justification before you are automatically disqualified.

Reminder: only 1 miss is allowed per term.

Google Classroom Related Questions
I received an email from google classroom about my assignment being “returned”. What does that mean?

After grading your assignment, proctors return it for you to have a confirmation that your assignment has been reviewed and graded.

How do I get credit after I complete viewing the online video courses?

All assignments must be “turned in” within google assignment to receive credit for assignment.

Why was my homework returned to me?
Please check the comments. If your homework was returned to you with a grade of a grade out of 100, e.g. "100/100," your grade has been documented and Google Classroom is letting you know. If you did not receive a grade, please check the comments section, and re-submit the assignment as per the instructions.
