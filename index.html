  • Assignments Related Questions

  • What are the due dates of assignments?

    Due dates are visible in your local time zone in google classroom per assignment. Optionally, you can click on google calendar to see assignment due dates from the classroom view.

  • What If there is a school commitment that prevents me from completing assignments?

    According to VT internship policies, every intern must complete assignments on time or will be disqualified. However, it is an at-will internship which means you can gracefully leave by creating a help desk ticket. You can always apply for future internships.

  • When will VMware hands-on task assignments will start?

    Stay tuned for weekly assignments as it publishes every week to only qualified interns who advance to next week.

  • Are weekly LIVE meetings mandatory?

    No. LIVE meetings are another way of intern collaboration and getting your questions or doubts answered by support staff.

    You can choose either a proactive or reactive exception option.

  • Can I use my school email for signup to google classroom and communication?
