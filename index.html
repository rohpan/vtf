Assignments Related Questions
Due dates of assignments?

Assignments are due on Wednesday at 11:59 pm PST.

Due dates are also visible in your local time zone in google classroom per assignment. Optionally, you can click on google calendar to see assignment due dates from the classroom view.

What is the Weekly Certificate of Completion? What if unable to receive it on time?
It is UNGRADED and OPTIONAL assignment. It is for interns to claim a certificate for weekly efforts. You can share Certificates of Completion on social media. If you did not fill it and receive a Certificate of Completion before due date, there is no late processing for this.
1-day late submissions of due date?
When turning in your assignment late, you should state a reason in the comments section for the submission in Google Classroom. All LATE assignments incur a 10% penalty. You will be allowed 1 late assignment per term. After a 2nd late assignment, you will automatically be disqualified from the Internship and removed from Google Classroom and Discord.
Unable to submit VT assignments because school, person and any other reason?
According to VT internship policies, every intern must complete assignments on time or will be disqualified. However, it is an at-will internship which means you can gracefully leave by creating a help desk ticket. You are welcome to apply for future internships. If you have a MISSING graded assignment or did not submit proof-of-completion for any week during the internship, you will automatically be disqualified from the Internship and removed from Google Classroom and Discord.
Are weekly LIVE meetings mandatory?
No. LIVE meetings are another way of intern collaboration and getting your questions or doubts answered by support staff.
How can I get an extension on an assignment?
Refer to Q3 and Q4.
